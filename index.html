Release Date: Reebok Question Mid Answer To No One
Following the return of the Reebok Question Mid “Blue Toe” this past weekend, up next for the legendary silhouette from Reebok and A.I. is this “Answer To No One” colorway. This take on the Reebok Question Mid offers multicolor detailing across a chalk upper, a custom graphic sock-liner, classic “Q” and “3” heel logos, and other flavorful hits atop a chalk midsole and glass blue tinted outsole. The shoes will aoso release along matching apparel which includes a Vector Fleece Hoodie, Vector Fleece Joggers, and two graphic tee variations. Look for the Reebok Question Mid “Answer To No One” to release exclusively on reebok.com on September 1st for $160.
